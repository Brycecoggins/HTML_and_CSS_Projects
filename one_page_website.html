Who Are We?

We are a newly formed local non-profit organization committed to rescuing animals from the pound.
We pick them up off the street, when called about an animal, or adopt them from the pound.
We take them off the hands of overencumbered pet owners as well!
We have dogs, cats, birds, and others in all shapes and sizes.
We believe every animal deserves a loving home.
Please contact us if you are one of those!
